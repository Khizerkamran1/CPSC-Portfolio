DATA 605: Data Story — Data and Visual Analysis of Adidas (US) Quarterly Sales Data From 2020–20211 (July 2023)

Athletic footwear has witnessed increasing trends in recent years, driven by various factors such as lifestyle awareness, fashion trends, and technological advancements. This analysis explores the rising support for athletic footwear from Adidas for both men and women. By analyzing market reports and industry insights, it becomes evident that Adidas has capitalized on these trends to establish itself as a prominent brand in the athletic footwear market.




DATA 607: Analysis of Canadian Turbine Energy Generation Data (April 2023)

The Canadian Wind Turbine Database contains the geographic location and key technology details for wind turbines installed in Canada. This database consists of 15 columns and 6,698 rows of which all describe recorded information regarding the types of turbines installed within various geographical locations in Canada alongside also describing the power that these various types of turbines generate (in kW).
